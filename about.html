ABOUT US

THE PLAN

AS Realty, headquartered in the vibrant city of Visakhapatnam, stands as a premier real estate development and leasing firm, renowned for our expertise in commercial, retail, and industrial properties. Founded over three decades ago, AS Realty has grown from a visionary concept into a powerhouse in the real estate sector. Our journey began with a single project, and today, we proudly manage a diverse portfolio that spans millions of square feet. With a steadfast commitment to quality, innovation, and delivering world-class, sustainable assets, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry. Our growth story is a testament to our unwavering dedication to excellence, strategic planning, and the trust our clients place in us. Looking ahead, we are poised to expand across India, maintaining our high standards and commitment to sustainability in every project we undertake.
